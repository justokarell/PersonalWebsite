SAL HATES WASTE

Overview

[Github] If a restaurant or cafe has surplus food that they want to donate, Salhateswaste (Sal) is a website where they can find and connect with local nonprofits like shelters that are looking for their donations.
Let's say you work at a coffee shop that has 10-30 bagels leftover at the end of every day. Instead of calling a bunch of nonprofits you found on google maps, you can search our map of local nonprofits that are actively searching for your donations.
